About Us

Welcome to Mani Cafe

Located in the beautiful coastal town of Nagapattinam, Tamil Nadu, Mani Cafe has been serving authentic South Indian cuisine since 2010. Our cafe combines traditional recipes with a cozy atmosphere to create memorable dining experiences.

We source our ingredients locally and prepare everything fresh daily. From our aromatic filter coffee to our crispy dosas, every dish is made with love and attention to detail.

Whether you're a local or visiting our beautiful town, we invite you to experience the warmth of South Indian hospitality at Mani Cafe.
